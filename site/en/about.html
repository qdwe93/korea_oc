Greeting

Message from the CEO

Thank you for visiting KOREA OIL CHEMICAL CO.,LTD.

Based in Choryang at the gateway of the Port of Busan, we specialize in the distribution of liquid fuels and related products, growing together with partners in Korea, Japan, China and beyond.

Three principles guide everything we do. First, trust: a promise made is a promise kept. Second, expertise: our experience in fuel and chemical distribution lets us design the right supply solution for each client. Third, a can-do spirit: whatever the request, we start from "yes, we can" and find a way.

We regard the trust of each and every client as our greatest asset, and we will continue to repay it with unwavering sincerity.

Seo Yong-hwan, CEO — KOREA OIL CHEMICAL CO.,LTD
