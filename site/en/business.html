Our Business

Reliable energy supply for industry, at sea and on shore.

Liquid Fuel Distribution

We distribute the liquid fuels that keep industrial sites running. Supply is tailored to each client's operating environment and volume, delivered on time with honest, specification-compliant quality. From spot orders to regular contracted deliveries, terms are flexible.

  • Heavy fuel oil (Bunker C)

  • Other industrial fuel oils

Marine & Port Supply

Located at Choryang, the gateway to the Port of Busan, we respond to fuel demand from vessels and port operations. Fast coordination aligned with berthing schedules supports shipping lines, agencies and port operators.

  • Fuel for port & logistics sites

  • Other chemical products
